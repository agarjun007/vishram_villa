Services by vishram village

Vishram village is located at Edava near Varkala. This picturesque resort is set on a hill along the Hariharapuram-lake banks. It takes a15 minute boatride from the lake to reach the the sand beach of Kapill and is at a distance of four kilometres from Varkala Space of relaxation and well-being. Our accomodations are designed to gel with natural surroundings and in accordance with Vastushastra. It combines local, recycled materials with modern aesthetics and furnishings . . All bungalows are air conditioned and equipped with fans on verandas and in the rooms. King size beds ,double or separated is available on request. Verandas overlook a private garden with a Lake view. . Hot water is available 24/7. Rooms are equipped with LCD television with satellite channels, and standard ECE power. Multicuisine restaurant ,ayurveda centre,country boat,yoga centre,travel desk, 24 hours security  are the facilties available in the resort.
